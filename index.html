Orthopaedic Conditions We Treat

Comprehensive care for orthopaedic conditions affecting infants, children, and adolescents.

Cerebral Palsy

Movement and muscle tone disorders often addressed with surgical or non-surgical interventions.

Foot Deformity

Includes clubfoot and other congenital/acquired foot misalignments managed with casting or surgery.

Fractures & Trauma

Expert care for broken bones, sports injuries, and trauma in children and teens.

Gait Abnormalities

Assessment and treatment of walking difficulties, toe walking, and limping patterns.

Hip Disorders

Conditions like DDH and Perthes disease are addressed with early screening and treatment.

Limb Deformity

Correction of bowlegs, knock knees, and limb length differences to ensure proper alignment.

Neuromuscular Disorders

Including muscular dystrophy and spinal conditions that affect muscle tone and posture.

Sports Injuries

Managing overuse, sprains, growth plate injuries, and return-to-play rehabilitation.

Meet Dr. Pule

Dr. Pule completed his MBBS at the University of Botswana Faculty of Medicine in 2014. He pursued his postgraduate studies in Orthopaedic Surgery in Kenya through the College of Surgeons of East, Central and Southern Africa (COSECSA) and Moi University, graduating in 2021.

After completing his M.Med (Ortho) and FCS (ECSA), Dr. Pule undertook a one-year Orthopaedic Trauma Fellowship (focused on pelvis/acetabulum trauma) at the University of Cape Town, endorsed by the South African Orthopaedic Association (SAOA), which he completed in December 2022.

In 2025, he further advanced his expertise through a SAOA-endorsed Paediatric Orthopaedic Surgery Fellowship at the University of Stellenbosch, completed in December 2025.

Dr. Pule is an active affiliate of the Botswana Surgical Society, South African Orthopaedic Association (SAOA), and the South African Paediatric Orthopaedic Society (SAPOS). He is passionate about lifelong learning and remains engaged in congresses and training in the evolving field of Orthopaedics.
